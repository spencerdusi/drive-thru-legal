Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Drive-Thru Delay Index ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

1) Information We Collect

  • Account information: email address, authentication provider identifiers, and user ID.
  • Location data: your device location when you grant permission.
  • User-submitted content: wait-time reports, optional notes, and estimated cars in line.
  • Technical metadata: timestamps, basic app events, and service logs used for reliability and abuse prevention.

2) How We Use Information

  • To provide drive-thru wait-time predictions and community reporting features.
  • To secure accounts and prevent spam or abuse.
  • To operate, troubleshoot, and improve app performance.
  • To respond to support requests.

3) How Information Is Shared

We use third-party infrastructure providers to operate the app, including Firebase services for authentication and database functionality. We do not sell your personal information.

4) Data Retention and Deletion

You can request deletion of your account directly in the app from the Profile screen. When deletion completes, we remove your authentication account and related user data from our primary app database.

Some limited operational logs or backups may persist for a short period for security, legal, or disaster-recovery purposes and are automatically removed according to service retention windows.

5) Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6) Your Choices

  • You can choose whether to grant location permissions.
  • You may use the app in guest mode for browsing (with limited features).
  • You can contact us to request help with your data.

7) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date and post the revised policy at this URL.
